# Port Rib Corn Soup

## info  
* 30 minutes
* 5 servings  

## ingredients
* pork ribs
* 2 corn
* 4 carrots
* red dates
* goji berries
* green onion


## steps  
1. Place blanched and cleaned pork ribs onto soup pot
2. Add corn, carrots and red dates
3. Add water and simmer on low heat for 30 minutes
4. Add Chinese yam and salt continue to simmer for 20 minutes
5. Add goji berries and green onion
